About Ants

Ants are among the most successful creatures on Earth, with over 12,000 known species.

They live in complex colonies that can contain millions of individuals working together.

Some ant colonies can live for decades, with queens living up to 30 years!

Colony Structure

The Queen

The colony's founder and primary egg-layer.

Workers

Sterile females who forage, defend, and care for the young.

Soldiers

Specialized workers with large heads and powerful jaws for defense.

Males

Short-lived individuals whose primary role is to mate with new queens.
